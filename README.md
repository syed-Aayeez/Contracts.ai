# Contracts.ai 

**AI-Powered Contract Intelligence for Software Asset Management (SAM)**

Contracts.ai is an intelligent legal and administrative assistant designed to revolutionize how businesses manage their software licenses and vendor contracts. By leveraging advanced AI, the platform automates the extraction of key metadata, identifies risks, and provides conversational Q&A capabilities directly linked to your documents.

## How to Use the Platform (BRING YOUR OWN GEMINI API KEY TO START THE PLATFORM)

1. **Upload a Contract:** Navigate to the **Document Library** on the left panel and drag-and-drop your PDF file (e.g., a vendor agreement or exercise guide).
2. **Wait for Processing:** The system will quickly ingest the document and display it in the viewer.
3. **Analyze:** 
   * Go to the **SAM Intelligence** tab on the right.
   * Click on any of the **Quick Analysis** options (like *Audit & True-Up Risks*) to generate an instant report.
4. **Ask Questions:** Switch to the **AI Assistant** tab. Type a specific question about your document in the chat box.
5. **Export Data:** Use the **Generation Tools** to download your insights as a Knowledge Graph, Excel spreadsheet, or PowerPoint presentation.
6. **Toggle Language (Optional):** Use the top navigation bar to switch the interface between English and العربية (Arabic).

## Key Features

### 1. Intelligent Document Library
**Drag & Drop Upload:** Easily upload Software Asset Management (SAM) agreements, SaaS contracts, or vendor documents in PDF format.
**Centralized Hub:** View document metadata, page counts, and file sizes at a glance.

### 2. Conversational AI Assistant
**Smart Q&A:** Chat directly with your contracts. Ask questions like, "Summarize this document,"* or *"What are the renewal terms?"
**Source-Backed Citations:** The AI doesn't just answer; it cites specific page numbers from your uploaded PDFs, allowing you to instantly verify the information.

### 3. Quick Analysis (SAM Intelligence)
Generate one-click automated reports for critical business metrics:
**License Entitlements:** Extract quantities and user limits.
**Audit & True-Up Risks:** Proactively identify hidden financial risks and compliance clauses.
**Renewal Timeline:** Track critical dates and deadlines.
**Executive Summary:** Get a high-level overview of complex agreements.
**Pricing Comparison:** Analyze cost structures across different vendor documents.

### 4. Advanced Generation Tools
**Knowledge Graph:** Automatically generate visual maps of relationships within the contract (e.g., policies, users, licensing, roles).
**Export Metadata:** Download extracted contract data directly into an Excel (`.xlsx`) format for seamless inventory management.
**Exec. Presentation:** Automatically compile findings into a ready-to-present PowerPoint (`.pptx`) summary.
